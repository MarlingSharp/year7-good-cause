Why it's Bad

Greenhouse gases cause global warming by trapping heat in themselves and making the

environment hotter. They can also contribute to diseases from smog and air pollution.

Finally, it can also cause extreme weather, loss of food, increased wildfires, worse

hurricanes and glaciers melting.
