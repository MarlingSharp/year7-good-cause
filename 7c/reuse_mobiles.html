How can we reuse our old mobile phones?

Back to Home

Every year, people throw away millions of mobile phones as most phones aren't built to last, and manufacturers are constantly encouraging us to buy new ones. But there are many ways to repair and reuse old phones in useful ways which will save you money too!

For example, you could try turning your old mobile into a camera, perfect for taking pictures. Alternatively, you could turn it into an alarm clock to wake you up in the morning or even a TV remote if you have installed the correct apps!

So, with that in mind, there are many ways to reuse your old phones in new and inventive ways. The mobile phone companies may perhaps encourage you to simply throw your old phone away, but in reality there are in fact many ways to reuse an old phone. Just be inventive and you'll find many uses for them!
