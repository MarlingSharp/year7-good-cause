Messaging services

Messaging services like Whatsapp, Messages from Apple or Samsung and even Discord can help you keep in touch with loved ones and friends and are really helpful when it comes to contacting people when far away, but can make bullying worse as the bully cannot see the victim and cannot know how emotionally hurt the victim is and so they may carry on bullying the victim.

Video calling

Video calling services like Google Meet, Zoom and Facetime can be really helpful in seeing and interacting with loved ones and friends especially during the pandemic that we are in right now but can be a hard job setting up first time and can be awkward if you are not expecting one and are occupied.

Public Social Media

Facebook

Facebook is a social chatting service where people post things that other users can reply to and react to. The posts can vary from chat about everyday life to cute cat pictures.

Instagram

Instagram is a social platform that people post usually about their most recent youtube videos or day to day life.

TikTok

Tiktok is a social platform that users post videos that others can like, comment and share. Some people get depressed when they don't get enough likes or comments and others get bullied for being overweight, ugly or just annoying.

Twitter

Twitter is a social platform that users post tweets about almost everything from news to games and most users are either youtubers or followers keeping up to date with their favourite youtubers.

Snapchat

Snapchat is a service where users message and share their account QR code with others and is mostly known for the filters they provide.
