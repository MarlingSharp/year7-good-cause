The Benefits Of Water

Most people in England get clean water straight from the tap and it's always clean. Every single time. In Africa the potential threat in drinking water in some countries.

The threat of drinking water in Africa is major. It can cause diseases like diarrhoea, cholera, dysentery, typhoid, polio and stomach aches.

How Clean Water Helps

Clean water helps people in LOADS of ways. It keeps them hydrated and makes them live longer because their body isn't flooded with dirty water. Clean water can help you become more hygenic. Here at Water For Africa we believe that everyone should have the right to drink CLEAN and SAFE WATER.
