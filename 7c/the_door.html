What is The Door?

The Door is a charity in stroud that help young people with issues they may have like going to school after lockdown one of their parents dying or fights in the family.

If you area parent they can talk to you if you don't know what your child is feeling or if you are a child they have youth groups and youth clubs, there are also youth ambassadors who you can call.
